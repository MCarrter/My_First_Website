Pokemon GO! Community Day History!

Pokemon GO Community Days have been out since January of 2018! It will be celebrating its 10th Community Day this October!

Each Community Day lasts 3-hours, where the specefic Pokemon of the day will spawn in large amounts, with the chance of it being shiny!

Special Moves can also be learned in this time span, usually by evolving the Pokemon during the event

This page shows all of the past Community Days and what Pokemon were celebrated on each day!

An article of each Community Day is linked to each promotional image of its respective day if you want to learn more!
